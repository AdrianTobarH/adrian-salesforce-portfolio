Case Automation Engine

Business Problem

Support teams were manually assigning cases and updating status transitions, causing delays and inconsistent SLA tracking.

Solution

Designed Flow-based automation to manage assignment routing, status lifecycle transitions, and escalation triggers automatically.

Architecture

  • Record-triggered Flow
  • Assignment Rules
  • Validation Rules
  • Permission Sets

Impact

Reduced manual intervention and improved response time visibility across support workflows.
